Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

BurnItAway ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Worry Text: Text you enter for burning is processed locally and never stored or transmitted
  • Photos: Only accessed when you choose to burn them, then permanently deleted
  • App Preferences: Sound settings, reminder times, and daily usage counts

Usage Information

  • Daily burn count (stored locally)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Reminder notifications (if enabled)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core text burning and photo burning features
  • To send reminder notifications (if you enable them)
  • To track daily usage limits for free users

Information Storage

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: For subscription purchases and app distribution
  • Apple Photos: For photo access (with your explicit permission)
  • Apple Notifications: For reminder notifications (if enabled)

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security features and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal information.

Your Rights

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable notifications in app settings
  • Revoke photo library access in iOS Settings
  • Cancel subscriptions through Apple's subscription management

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: vaibhav@aayutech.in

This privacy policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices. Since we don't collect personal data, this policy is intentionally simple and focused on local device storage.
